Receptions

Temple Inn & Suites offers convenient and affordable options for wedding receptions. Our close proximity to the temples makes us the perfect choice for your out of town guests.

Getting to and from the temple couldn't be easier! Shuttle busses leave on a regular schedule in line with temple sessions. Wedding parties can also reserve a bus for their wedding party staying on site.

Our spacious reception hall can accommodate up to 300 guests. We work with several event planners who can create the reception of your dreams.

Our in-house chefs and bakers have a combined 58 years of experience in the industry. Just about any menu item can be created for you.
