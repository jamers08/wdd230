Discover COLA City!

Riverbanks Zoo & Gardens

Riverbanks Zoo & Garden is home to more than 2,000 beautiful animals and one of the nation's most stunning botanical gardens. The 170-acre site is full of natural habitat exhibits, scenic river views, spectacular valley overlooks and significant historic landmarks.

Richland Library

Richland Library offers state-of-the-art technology and a variety of literary and cultural programs, resources, and information. There are 13 facilities located throughout Richland County, so find your closest branch today!

State and Local Parks

Columbia offers a variety of state and local parks for community enjoyment. If you want a nature hike, a picnic lunch, or a stroll along the river, you have plenty of options to find beauty, history, and fun in the great outdoors!

South Carolina State Capital

Located at the heart of Downtown Columbia, the State House offers both guided and self-guided tours to learn about the history and architecture of this amazing building.

South Carolina Department of Archives and History

If history is your thing, Columbia has it in spades. Government records going back to the colony's beginning in 1670 can be found at the State Department of Archives and History.
